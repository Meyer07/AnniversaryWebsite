December 2024

The Day We Met

We actually met twice, the very first time we met was in the Cambridge Commons at UWM, she was hanging out with two friends Gabe and Tedros. Sam and a friend at the time Alex were walking by and saw them playing Magic: The Gathering and stopped in and played for a little with Tedros and Gabe, She was playing Minecraft on her phone. Sam remembers walking in and seeing her and thinking she was pretty cute, They exchanged snapchats and didn't really speak until a year or so later. The night they did meet again was a week after Sam turned 20 years old and wanted to celebrate with his friend Cade, the two of them plus Cade's at the time girlfriend made their way to her house in order to not worry about the RA's. Maggie was napping when Sam had arrived and by the time she woke up, Sam was really really drunk and eventually threw up blood in her bathroom and so Maggie,Cade and Cade's Girlfriend took Sam to Ascension Hospital on North Ave. All four stayed at the hospital till 5am where Maggie got to know a little bit about Sam including the fact he was from Green Bay and knew the Denmark was a small village 20mins south of Green Bay. She also thought that Sam was really funny.
